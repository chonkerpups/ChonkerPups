Goodest Boy

You have a 10% chance of minting a Goodest Boy, each with unique traits, including a goodness level from Highest: Super Good, Really Good, Kinda Good, with Well Trained being lowest. The higher the ranking:

  • The higher the portion of $TOKEN that the Goodest Boy earns from taxes.
  • The higher chance of stealing a newly minted Chonker Pup or Goodest Boy.
